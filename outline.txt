Chapter 1 - 
Introduction: Nick Carraway moves to West Egg, establishing himself as a "highly moral" narrator who reserves judgment while introducing his fascination with Gatsby.
The Dinner: Nick visits the aristocratic East Egg to dine with his cousin Daisy and her arrogant husband, Tom, whose mistress interrupts the evening with a phone call.
The Contrast: The evening highlights the tension between the "new rich" of West Egg and the "old money" socialites who are bored and cynical.
The Sighting: Upon returning home, Nick sees the mysterious Gatsby for the first time, reaching out toward a distant green light across the water.
